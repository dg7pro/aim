Portal Options

The portal options let you reset passwords for visitors, move visitor tickets from one e-mail account to another, export data or enable/disable login access.

Reset Portal Password

Here you can reset visitor ticket portal passwords. An option to generate a password is provided to help with more secure passwords. If you choose to send the email, visitor gets notification of the password change.

Move Tickets to Another E-Mail Account

Here you can move visitor tickets from one email address to another. Visitors can change their email at any time in their account area. If you choose to send the email, visitor gets notification of the change

Export Portal Data to CSV

Export visitor data to csv for batch mailing purposes.

Enable/Disable Login Access

Enable/disable login access for visitor ticket portal. Useful if you think the system is being abused by someone.
